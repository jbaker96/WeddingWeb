GETTING THERE

From the WEST

This is how the Bride and Groom will be coming out.

It is a 9 hour drive from Vancouver (+ a ferry if you live on the island)

We know this is the longest way but those that choose this way will enjoy the beautiful drive through the Rockies we do every year.


From the EAST

Either flying in to or coming from Calgary, it is about a 3 hour drive.


From the NORTH

This can be done two ways, either through the prairies and then head west for a 10 hour drive.

Or straight down through Jasper for a 9 our drive.


From the SOUTH

Another option is to fly to Cranbrook which is an hour south of Invermere.

Give us a heads up if you choose this option and we will contact you with others on how to get up to Invermere


From the AIR

For those invitees that are aviation trained, Invermere has a fully serviced airport.

The airport code for Invermere is CYQX

Those that choose to fly in directly, we can coordinate vehicles once you have arrived in the valley so you aren't stranded.
